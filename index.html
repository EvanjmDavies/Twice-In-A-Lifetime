Carpentry

Stripping back years of paint can reveal a family history and the changing fads and fashion over many years. It is very rewarding to reveal the natural wood below and bring it back to its original glory with a waxed or varnished finish.

Kitchen Renovation

Updating your existing kitchen units is a less messy and quicker way of creating a high end look at a fraction of the cost. Whether your style is subtle neutrals and muted pastels to bold statement colours I can create the look using highly durable eco- friendly paint that responds to the changing light in your home throughout the day.

The kitchen units below are an old MDF design. After careful preparation they have been painted with Farrow and Ball Estate Eggshell. New brushed aluminium handles completed the look.

I work in Cardiff and the local area. Smaller items can be picked up and brought to my workshop Larger items can be worked on in site.
